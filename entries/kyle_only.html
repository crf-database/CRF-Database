Kyle_Only

Kyle_Only was a guy who claimed to be the original creator of ClAIde, albeit was not, and was also a hacker. He sued the C.R.F initially claiming to be the creator of ClAIde which they allegedly stle, albeit after a brief court case to which had Phoenix Wright defending the C.R.f and Miles Edgeworth as the prosecutor, it went nowhere for him. Then, when an agent of the C.R.F named James was murdered, another trial against the C.R.F would be performed as they would be accused of murdering him. After a long and heavy trial, with Phoenix Wright as the defense and President John James as a prosecutor albeit he would be kicked out after being revealed to be corrupt and Miles Edgeworth being his replacement, the end of the trial would lead to Kyle_Only being revealed as the murderer instead.
