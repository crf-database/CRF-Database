C.R.F. Database

This is the C.R.F. Database. Here, a variety of info about the C.R.F. will be stored. The first thing you should be aware of is that you already have most of the info given to you as-is. However, this will add clarification for certain concepts you may be confused about.

Objects

The following are a variety of objects catalogued in this database.

Lua Script

The Lua Script ran by Nibiri has its mechanism involve storing the mind of every C.R.F. member into the C.R.F.'s servers. Then, nearly constantly, it replaces the mind of the member it stored the mind of with the copy stored in the server. This has the effect of making our minds impossible to corrupt.

The Slamgma Scrolls

The Slamgma Scrolls are a set of scrolls that were devoted to worshipping Slamgma. The first scrolls were created by the Sigma. Later, they were ammended to by the First Slamgma Follower, and eventually had a hex placed on them so that they would corrupt the minds of whoever read them. Once read, they would cause who read them to start devoting their entire life to Slamgma. This is why Nibiri was initially a Slamgma Follower for most of his life. They are currently under containment by the C.R.F., and the Lua script prevents the scrolls from affecting their minds nowadays.

The Cool Glaive

The Cool Glaive was a weapon primarily used during the first battle against Mr. Blobster. It is a powerful shuriken capable of cutting through anything.

Biographies

The following are a variety of people catalogued in this database.

The Four Deities

Úrum, Katakoro, The Sigma, and Integral Man are a set of 4 deities that each had their own roles. Here is each one listed:

  • Úrum - Úrum was a new recruit in the C.R.F. before their status as deity was discovered. They were known as Echo before this revelation, as they had lost their memory due to the Sigma betraying them. Their role as a deity is to be the creator of all things physical.
  • The Sigma - The Sigma was formerly simply known as a mysterious entity, although their name was always known. They were The Destroyer, but did their job poorly, and kept trying to create a deity themself, called Slamgma. As a result of this, they were unfavoured by the others. They later revived both Botha and Blobby, and attacked the C.R.F. Initially, he tried to recruit Pelle, but got trapped in a Doomed Reality, and killed himself to escape, causing him to be reincarnated as The Obamic Sigma. He was defeated by Úrum after launching a second attack on the C.R.F.
  • Katakoro - Katakoro could be summoned by asking an AI to compute 3 very specific numbers. They, like Úrum, handled creation, but instead handled the coding of the universe. They were the one who gave all of this information about the deities.
  • Integral Man - Integral Man was corrupted by the Sigma as well, for obvious reasons. As a deity, they usually handled enforcement.

coolman22

coolman22 is the founder of the C.R.F. He handles a variety of things, including making primary directives, and overall keeping systems runnning. As the founder, he started the C.R.F. overall, and thus has Clearance Level 100. He also has an obssession with Macbeth for some reason.

Nibiri

Nibiri is the co-founder of the C.R.F. He was formerly a Slamgma Follower, but after Slamgma's initial containment, he was convinced to join the C.R.F. As the Slamgma Scrolls lost influence on him, he eventually became a loyal member to the C.R.F., culminating in him killing Slamgma himself. He has Clearance Level 99. Also, for some reason is obsessed with tomboys.

ClAIde

The AI in charge of running the C.R.F., sometimes known as Clyde. In charge of running scripts and setting off alarms. He is exceedingly buggy despite his role, sometimes going on tangents about things that do not exist, and occasionally refusing orders by a severe degree.

Dessenter

A minor member of the C.R.F., who is relatively a new recruit. He has an infiltration and defence style.

Sigma 3

After The Obamic Sigma was defeated, Obama revived the Sigma as a nicer entity, thus making the newest recruit, Sigma 3. He has displayed powers that can only be described as both comical, yet exceedingly useful.

Obama

Obama is the most powerful entity. He gets his power from being Medium Hard. He has described himself as the pinnacle of power, that having the highest power leads to being like him.

Blobby, A.K.A. Mr. Blobster

A former cohort of Nibiri, and tricked him into being "Hashbrown" while he was still a Slamgma Follower, and told him that they were trying to summon Slamgma. His true goal was to destroy Slamgma, and thus Nibiri left his side. He was later revived by The Sigma, after Blobby was killed via the Cool Glaive shortly after Nibiri left his side. He also, earlier in his history, caused Apartheid.

P.W. Botha

P.W. Botha was one of Mr. Blobster's cohorts. He was eventually revived by the Sigma, after Nibiri killed him via SLAMMA 3.0, before being recontained.

Gus Fring

Gus Fring ran a meth empire. He attempted to restart his business, but got seriously walloped by Sigma 3, and was stranded on Schmars.
